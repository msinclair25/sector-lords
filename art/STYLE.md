# Sector Lords — Art Style Bible

Cyberpunk crime strategy. Dark night city, wet streets, neon magenta (#ff2bd6) and cyan (#2bf0ff) accents, deep purple-black shadows (#07060f). Stylized digital illustration (not photoreal). No readable text, logos, or watermarks in generated assets.

## Master prompt fragment

> Stylized cyberpunk digital illustration, night city rain, neon magenta and cyan rim light, dark moody palette, game art asset, no text, no watermark

## Consistency rules

1. Same neon pair and rain mood on every asset.
2. Portraits: bust or 3/4 figure, dramatic rim light, readable silhouette.
3. Tiles: top-down or slight isometric city block; leave center readable for owner tint.
4. Prefer WebP later; JPG ok for style-proof pack.
